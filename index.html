Our Mission

The Institute of Personal Financial Coaching is dedicated to developing knowledgeable, ethical, and practical financial coaches who help individuals and families make informed decisions about money, debt, saving, investing, retirement, and long-term financial security.

Professional Standards

Education

IPFC promotes strong foundational knowledge in personal finance, budgeting, investing, retirement planning, insurance, taxation, estate planning, and financial behavior.

Ethics

Certified professionals are expected to act with honesty, integrity, transparency, and respect for the individuals and families they serve.

Verification

IPFC maintains a certification verification system so individuals may confirm credential status and certification information.

Featured Credential

CPFP™

Certified Personal Financial Planner™

The CPFP™ designation recognizes individuals who have demonstrated competency in personal financial planning, coaching, retirement planning, investing fundamentals, debt management, risk management, taxation basics, and ethical financial education.

Certification Pathway

1. Complete Education

Applicants complete approved personal finance, investing, retirement, tax, insurance, and estate planning coursework.

2. Submit Proof

Applicants provide documentation of completed courses, certificates, or approved professional learning.

3. Earn Certification

Approved applicants receive a unique certification number and are added to the IPFC verification registry.
